Antigonish

Villain Info

Not much is known about the evil mastermind behind the destruction of your home world as well as your being sent to another universe. The only clue given to you of the true identity of this monster is a single recording of his laughter.

Boogie Man

Villain Info

There are many who have heard the name, but few who know the true tale of the Boogie-Man. Once a soldier in a war from a long ago. While checking a basement for hiding enemy soldiers he was exposed to a poison gas that sprang up from demonic symbols scrolled in the floor. After his death he was transformed into something else that warps the minds of those who come into contact with him as well as reality itself.
